# Car Sales Dashboard - Power BI

## Project Overview

The Car Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze and visualize car sales performance. The dashboard provides valuable insights into sales trends, revenue, customer behavior, and regional performance, helping businesses make data-driven decisions.

## Objectives

* Monitor overall car sales performance.
* Analyze revenue and profit trends.
* Identify top-performing car brands and models.
* Compare sales across different regions.
* Create interactive visualizations for business insights.

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Modeling
* Data Visualization

## Dashboard Features

* Total Sales KPI
* Total Revenue Analysis
* Car Brand Performance
* Model-wise Sales Analysis
* Regional Sales Distribution
* Monthly Sales Trends
* Interactive Filters and Slicers

## Key Insights

* Identified top-selling car brands and models.
* Analyzed revenue growth trends over time.
* Compared sales performance across regions.
* Created interactive reports for business decision-making.
